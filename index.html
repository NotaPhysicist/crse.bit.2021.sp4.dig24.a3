The Society for the Understanding of Important English Words, is a scientific DAO dedicated to the exploration of the meanings of important English words.

More about us.

The Society is an invitation-only, private organization of like-minded, globally distributed language scholars with the shared mission of promoting important words of the English language. And not just English words, but important words in all languages everywhere.

We're guilty of taking this a tiny bit too seriously. But here we are, and we love what we do: and we hope you'll stick around for a minute or two to view this experimental presentation.
